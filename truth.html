De Standaard - The truth is that we're trying
to sell you a newspaper.
Client: De standaard
Role: complete design
Deliverables: All design elements
Awards: Silver at Eurobest 2018

The truth is that this is an advertisement for De Standaard; that the title has been highlighted to get your attention. The truth is also that the more subscriptions we sell, the more we can tell you the truth. Because the truth is, you are already reading enough fake news and half-truths. The truth is that you are counting on us to present the truth to you, and we are counting on you to invest your time and money so that we can always seek the truth.
